Buy a Hat (Burning Ring of Fire 1)
Objective: buy a hat.
1) Find vending machine.
2) Select a hat.
3) Pop-up will give address and amount to send to.
4) Send the KC at a KTM. (Approve transactions with your wallet address, the amount, and your wallet key)
5) Receive Hat ID, redeem at vending machine, put on hat.
6) Remember the transaction Block number.
